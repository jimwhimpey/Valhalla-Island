I understand the desire to shower in the morning. It’s a nice, fresh way to wake yourself and start the day. I don’t understand only showering in the morning.

The day is when you get dirty. Bringing that dirt to bed then washing it off in the morning, just in time to get dirty again doesn’t make sense. The bed is clean, if you want to be clean the next day showering only at night is works because you don’t get dirty while you sleep.

If you hold water preservation goals and limit yourself to a single shower per day, the evening option has to be the way to go. Sleep clean, start the day clean.
