--- layout: post title: "Birthday Haul" ---

It was my birthday over the weekend and I was lucky to get some great presents from my loved ones.

Garmin Edge 705 from Kristin. Literally the best bike computer available. Speed, cadence, power, heart rate, altitude, maps and GPS. It has a ton of dashboard style display options and is easy to upload and analyse at Garmin Connect, a beautifully polished web application.

Airport Extreme from My Mum. Completely sick of my ISP provided, flakey piece of shit wireless router it’s great to finally get something reliable. The N protocol is more than noticeably faster when transferring big files. I’m yet to put it through its paces streaming HD video to the Xbox.

It’s Not About the Bike from Kristin. The amazing story of Lance Armstrong. A book I’ve wanted for a long time. I’m a quarter of the way in and can barely put it down.

50 Marathons 50 Days from My Sister. Just looking at the cover of this book is inspiring. This is the same guy that ran 260 consecutive miles over 76 hours.
