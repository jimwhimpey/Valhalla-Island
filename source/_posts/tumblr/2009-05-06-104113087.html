For someone obsessed with looking at bikes online Trek’s website satisfies where almost every other bike manufacturer website fails. It’s perfectly organised, easy to navigate, functional and most importantly, provides great photos in super high resolutions.

The one thing it’s missing is price. I understand that prices differ all over the world and Trek have an obligation to send you to their suppliers but even a USD RRP would complete the online window shopping experience.
