My Day, Yesterday, One Year Ago

I was reminded lately about Garrett Murray’s My Day, Yesterday Flickr pool I participated in. It was a fun exercise at the time (I find video and audio editing oddly soothing) but it’s especially interesting looking back. Just one year later my days are vastly different, I can’t believe I drove from home to one campus and back before getting the train to another and getting home after dark. More than 3 hours of travel for just 3 hours of class!

I’m someone who finds it difficult to interrupt what I’m doing to take photos or record video in day to day life so I have little record (beyond memories) of what life was like at any one time. Dedicating a day every year or so to something like this could really be valuable to someone like me.
