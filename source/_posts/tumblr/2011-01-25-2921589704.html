--- layout: post title: "Leave" ---

Instead of public holidays I get an extra two weeks leave a year which accrues quickly. In the last two years I’ve taken just under four weeks of leave and three of those I had to take for rehabilitation following my bike accident. That means one week of recreational leave in two years, not much.

Contrary to what that might seems I’m not at all a workaholic and I think that’s the reason I feel so little compulsion to take leave.

I enjoy my job and while at work, I work hard but when my eight hours are up I go home and lead a rich life outside in my “down” time. While others either hate their jobs or work themselves into the ground to the point of desperately needing leave to rejuvenate and attend to their recreational lives, I maintain the balance.
