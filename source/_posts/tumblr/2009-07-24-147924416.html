--- layout: post title: "Harry Potter Movies" ---

I remember not enjoying the Harry Potter movies I had seen but after getting my movies up to date with the books I’ve read over the past few days I realised what’s missing.

Each Harry Potter book has a primary plot, whatever’s in the title, The Prisoner of Azkaban, The Goblet of Fire, etc. Problem is, the main story isn’t all that interesting. What is interesting is the day to day life of witches and wizards. I care little about fighting dementors but I’m intrigued by the invisibility cloak. The constrained format of a feature film means less time on the superfluous albeit interesting details.
