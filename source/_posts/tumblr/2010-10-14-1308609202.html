Some observations on Sony’s Google TV remote:

  • The yellow, blue, red and green buttons also have text labels (does anyone know what those ambiguous buttons on a remote even do!?)
  • Despite having a full keyboard the eject button requires the function modifier key (and sits in a curious position).
  • Lots of things require modifier keys.
  • There are four modifier keys.
  • There are two power buttons.
