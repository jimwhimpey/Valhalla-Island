--- layout: post title: "My Priorities When Choosing What to Eat" ---

In the order of vital to barely a factor.

  1. How much and how long it will take to wash up kitchenware used to prepare and consume the meal. Ideally the meal can be prepared and consumed with a single dish.
  2. How long it will take to prepare.
  3. If it requires cooking, how much attention does it require while cooking.
  4. Taste.
  5. Nutritional value.
  6. Variety. Both within a single meal and between meals I’ve eaten recently.
