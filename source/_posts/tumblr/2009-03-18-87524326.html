Pete and Brian are utterly brilliant. It’s been a long time since they’ve released something and this doesn’t disappoint. Every single one is perfectly acted, beautifully shot and produced. It seems they’ve uploaded a bunch of their old videos in high quality now too. Some of my old favourites are Showdown, Knock Knock, Pitch Meeting and the classic FCU: Fact Checkers Unit with Bill Murray.
