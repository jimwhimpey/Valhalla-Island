Every month or two I’m reminded about Quickflix, Australia’s answer to Netflix, and I go through this same process in my head:

  1. It’d be cool to watch more movies, maybe I should get a Quickflix membership.
  2. Do I really need to watch more movies? I’m already so busy and there’s so much other, more productive things I should and want to be doing.
  3. Stop kidding yourself, just relax and watch a fucking movie. Better to relax properly than spend hours getting nothing done in front of the computer. Then when you are on the computer, it’ll be productive time.
  4. You’re right but even though I have a nice big TV, my computer chair is more comfortable than my crappy couch anyway, why not just download movies and watch them on the computer?
  5. Then I forget about Quickflix.
