Australia’s new 24 hour free to air sports channel airs NASCAR races live pretty often. Up until last week I’d never had any exposure to NASCAR and I thought that the driving round in circles and making left turns thing was an exaggerated joke. Turns out, it’s not, they actually drive hundreds of laps around big ovals in different cities. As dumb as it seems I have to say that I respect their ability to do one thing and do it extremely well.
