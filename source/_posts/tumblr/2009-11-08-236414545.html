I love the use of diagrams to present information. For humour, to make a point, factually precise or a general approximation, I love it. Indexed is one my favourite websites.

So Fuck Yeah Venn Diagrams was an instant follow. But I’ve quickly been disappointed. There’s little clever distillation of information into graph form and a lot of forcing anything at all into a couple of circles.
