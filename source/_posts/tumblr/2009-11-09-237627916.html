For as long as the term “social media” has been around I haven’t had a clue what exactly it means. I can’t define it, no one I know, even those who use it regularly, can define it. Until now.

Social media is automatically posting a digest of your tweets to your blog.

And automatically sending your blog posts to your Twitter feed.

And automatically posting both to your Facebook page.

While clicking follow, the only manual part of the process.
