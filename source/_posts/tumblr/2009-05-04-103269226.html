Blood by The Middle East

Last night we were fortunate to see this beautiful song performed by The Middle East. Unfortunately it was the only song we saw. I’d never heard of them, the name put me off and the support often sucks. I couldn’t have been more wrong this time. This is exactly the kind of nuanced, thick music I love. I should’ve known Okkervil River would choose their support carefully.

Apologies for the audio quality, it’s hijacked from their MySpace (the band’s only website). While I mostly missed them this time I’ll catch them again when they play in June as part of a big East Coast of Australia tour. See them if you can.
