In 2007 I travelled through the US and was taken aback by the guilty-until-proven-innocent-style security measures. Three years and some failed terrorist plots later it’s reached soaring new heights of human-rights-defying invasiveness.

The latest “security upgrade” which provides a choice between a radiation emitting, naked body imaging backscatter machine or a full body pat down is bereft of respect for people’s privacy and dignity. Incredibly the TSA even requires US citizens to take part in these procedures upon entering the USA.

If I was a US citizen I’d be ashamed of my country. As long as this is the requirement for travelling to or within the US I won’t be back there.
