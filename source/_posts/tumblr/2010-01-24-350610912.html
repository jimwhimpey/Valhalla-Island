The way I see it, meat is the epitome of processed foods. Thousands of litres of water, fertiliser and months producing grain and produce that’s processed into feed for animals. Thousands more of litres of water, tons of grain and produce fed to cattle over their entire lifespan. Years after the process began the meat is processed into little packages ready to be transported to your supermarket.

We can eat what cattle eat, why not cut out the middle man animal?
