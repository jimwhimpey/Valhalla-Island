Giving it away is a weird choice. Are there people who would use Twitter, if only they could afford a mobile client app?

John Siracusa in response to the news Twitter has bought Tweetie for iPhone and is making it free.

The whole thing feels weird to me. Why would Loren Brichter abandon his massively successful independently run business, the freedom and control that comes with it to be another cog in the Twitter machine? It’d be a different story if Tweetie was languishing in obscurity but it’s got to be the most popular Twitter iPhone and Mac client. I guess they gave him a bag of money too big to refuse.

It’ll be interesting to see what happens to Tweetie for Mac, my favourite Twitter desktop client.
