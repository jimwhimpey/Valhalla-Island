In an extraordinary feat of endurance the first round Wimbledon match between American John Isner and Frenchman Nicolas Mahut has gone to an epic 59 games each in the 5th set. At a match time of 10 hours, it’s already three and a half hours longer than the second longest tennis match ever played.

And it’s not over yet. Play was unfortunately suspended due to poor light early this morning and will resume on day 4 of competition tonight.
