A good example of poor Microsoft UI design in the screenshots from the upcoming Office 2011 for Mac.

  1. Labelling each group of toolbar items results in identical labels being repeated within 40 pixels of each other.
  2. The groups don’t need labels in the first place.

Yet they remain. It’s the accumulation of details like this which make a many Microsoft application UIs bad and many Mac application UIs good.
