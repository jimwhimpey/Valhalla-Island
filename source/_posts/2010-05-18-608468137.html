Starting today, I’m joining BankSimple as a co-founder, with the role of Chief Product & Technology Officer. In a nutshell, I’m going to make sure we build something that’s simple, beautiful, and works really really well.

This is really exciting even though it’s unlikely BankSimple will stretch outside the US.

Industries like banking are ripe for smart people like Alex Payne to come in and do things right. Fundamentally banking is a simple concept and if you have the capital to begin with surely it’s possible to make a lot of money without the need to gouge your customers or skimp on things like support and a really good website.

Next please: insurance, real estate, air travel, postal service, supermarkets, etc.

Sidenote: Why do all names of things now have to be a single camel cased “word”? Why is BankSimple “BankSimple” and not the much more aesthetically pleasing and grammatically correct “Bank Simple”?
