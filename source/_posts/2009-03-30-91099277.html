--- layout: post title: Identity Theft in Car Design ---

I love how Audi frames their brand as the rebel, the individual, the enemy of mediocrity and convention.

Jason Fried before linking to this commercial for the Audi Q5.

The way it’s been shot with fleeting split second images of the cars, the Audi black and the rest silver make it difficult to actually compare the cars. Here’s a side by side with the Audi Q5 and Lexus RX which from what I can tell is the other car featured in the commercial.

Sure, Audi markets themselves as a rebel, the enemy of convention but the truth is they’re making the same boring, almost identical cars as every other manufacturer.
