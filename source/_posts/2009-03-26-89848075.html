Which brings me to the problem with this campaign. Who on earth would become a Comcast enthusiast? Who hangs out at a Comcast Web site and rearranges pretend furniture? Or, put more broadly: What sane person forms a loving attachment to a cable behemoth?

Slate on Comcast’s meaningless and somewhat creepy dream big commercial.

The marketing department and rest of the company work in opposition. The company’s products fail and the service sucks. Consumers hate the company. Nothing but bad experiences come to mind when thinking of the company. What’s the solution? Make a fucking commercial full of people in a miniature animated world singing about how great the company is. Finish it with ‘DREAM BIG’, a slogan contrived and meaningless in any situation let alone when related to a cable provider.

Ditch the bullshit marketing, invest the money in great service and making a great product and the marketing will take care of itself.
