Aaron Swartz's how to read more books

Paraphrased:

  1. Don’t read anything that’s not a book.
  2. Get books from the library.
  3. Don’t have friends, other hobbies or interests.
  4. Don’t fall asleep.

Reading 100 books a year is easy when you don’t do anything else.
