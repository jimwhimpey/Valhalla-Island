The Mini Moke, now there’s an innovative automobile. Use less materials, less electronics, strip it to its bare essentials and you have a car cheap to buy and cheap to run that even today is more practical and spacious than your modern sedan. It’s a shame they no longer make these, judging by the hugely inflated prices they go for second hand I’m sure there’s a market for them.
