Garrett Murray:

But here’s the thing, folks: I’ve been complaining for years that Tumblr is free. I don’t want it to be free. I want to pay for it. Tumblr is one of the few services on earth that refuses to take my money.

I feel exactly the same way. Money in exchange for reliability is a much better deal than free with downtime. I truly don’t understand the desire to give something away for free at any cost and when it’s at the cost of actually providing the thing you want to provide for free the whole situation is absurd.

This reminds me of something I wrote a year ago about This American Life begging the listeners of their free podcast to donate money to offset the huge cost of providing the podcast for free. It don’t make no damn sense.
