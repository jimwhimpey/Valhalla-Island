Fever Feed Reader

From the same dude who makes the excellent Mint website stats tool and designed around the same self hosted model. The self-hosted model works for Mint because needing it almost always means you’re already running similar software on your own server. For a standalone application like a feed reader I think it’s much less of a good idea. Even I, someone with experience in and totally comfortable with fucking around on a web server, don’t want to deal with the hassle of self-hosting an application. For anyone less technically equipped it could be a frustratingly difficult process to simply get it up and running.

Freeing myself of the burden of managing a self-hosted WordPress install was the number one reason I switched to Tumblr. I’ve been a much happier blogger ever since.
