I couldn’t believe it this morning watching 7 Early News that this was being reported as an iPhone virus with no more qualification than that. It requires three things:

  1. A jail broken phone.
  2. OpenSSH installed, not a default on most jail broken phones.
  3. The default root password.

So about 0.1% of iPhones whose users have gone out of their way to open up completely but haven’t taken the care to change their password are susceptible. Meanwhile every iPhone owner is under the impression their phones are vulnerable.

Don’t let the truth get in the way of a good story. Not that anyone having their iPhone background set to a picture of Rick Astley is a good story.
