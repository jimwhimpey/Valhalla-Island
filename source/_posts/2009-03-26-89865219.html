How to be Jason Bourne

A series of excerpts from the book Emergency: This Book Will Save Your Life:

“I’m looking at islands in the north, around Iceland, because no one will think of looking for anyone there,” Spencer said, his thick lips spreading into a self-satisfied smile. “If I can get some other B people [billionaires] to go there with me, we can build underground homes and use geothermal energy.”

It’s a gimmicky title but judging by the excerpts, it sounds great. The problem with books (or websites or guides or classes) on this subject is that they’re based heavily on secrets which cease to be secrets once they’re published internationally. A St Kitts passport might have worked wonders a few years ago but what happens when tens of thousands of American citizens start applying for them?
