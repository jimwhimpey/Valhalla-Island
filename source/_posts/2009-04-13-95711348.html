I’m really enjoying Marco’s series on looking for an apartment to rent. Despite being on opposite sides of the planet I think we’re looking for a pretty similar type of place.

We’re a month off seriously looking for a place but from just a few hours browsing the listings on realestate.com.au and Domain the ridiculous “sales” tactics (or complete incompetence) of real estate agents is obvious. The amount of listings with a single photo of the view or the exterior of an apartment building and nothing else is incredible. Then there’s the listings without any photos at all or with photos so low in resolution and quality it’s impossible to tell what colour the carpet is or whether the floor’s carpeted at all.
