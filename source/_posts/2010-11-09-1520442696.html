First it was Flock now RockMelt is adding bullshit to browser chrome. I can’t even think of an analogy for adding a subset of a website to the chrome of software made for accessing the same complete websites as its primary functionality.

A car with another smaller car inside it that doesn’t go as fast or as far?

The list of features in the screenshot — share easily, connect with friends, search faster, keep up on news — are all things any browser can do today by simply visiting websites. It’s impossible to tell what those avatars down the left hand edge represent but why would you ever want a row of faces constantly staring back at you while you’re browsing?

Shit like this seems to be a momentum gaining trend of social media being treated as though it has inherent value. The “features” RockMelt adds to Chrome may not be useful and they may actually detract from the web browsing experience but because it’s social media it’s considered valuable.
