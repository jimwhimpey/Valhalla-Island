New Flickr Photo Pages

For six years Flickr has had the same frustratingly insufficient photo pages and navigation between them. Two months after I create my own thing to address the problem Flickr fixes every issue natively. Albeit currently as an opt-in beta.

At first I wasn’t worried, the default size was still too small and navigation was still too slow. Then I saw the incredible zoom option with big photos, dark background, super fast keyboard navigation and loading times. It completely blows Darkroom away and it’s available on every kind of Flickr output.

My happiness as a Flickr user outweighs my disappointment as Darkroom developer.

C’est la vie.
