--- layout: post title: "IBM Centennial Film: They Were There" --- IBM Centennial Film: They Were There

A compelling short movie directed by Errol Morris with gorgeous music by Phillip Glass to commemorate IBM’s 100th birthday. IBM is an incredibly vast and eclectic company, I think of them as a hyperactive Apple. It’s full of great quotes like this:

And now barcodes are everywhere.

Well worth the 30 minutes. Via Daring Fireball
