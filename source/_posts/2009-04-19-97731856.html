I hate this vigilante journalism, the-media-is-lying-to-you bullshit from people with a copy of Flash, a microphone and a week alone in their bedroom.

Here’s his premise without the lame jokes and superfluous animation:

  1. Somali pirates hijack ships at gunpoint, receive a ransom and leave.
  2. The media reports this.
  3. Foreigners ditch nuclear waste and overfish Somalia’s waters.
  4. The media doesn’t mention this when reporting on pirate attacks.
  5. So who’s the real bad guys?!

Yeah, committing a crime against an innocent company and group of people is justified when a totally unrelated group of people pollute and overfish your waters. According to this guy, it’s not only justified but the pirates are the good guys.

Then the terrible media only reports the pirate attacks without going in depth on every story relating to Somalia and the whole continent it belongs to.

The mainstream media sucks in a lot of ways, this isn’t one of them.
