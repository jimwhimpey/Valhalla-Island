Photos Inside the Paris Metro

Incredible photos taken by a bunch of testosterone-fuelled adrenaline junkies sneaking into the Metro, Paris’s 100-year-old underground railway service. This stuff fascinates the hell out of me and it’s worth reading through the bravado to get an idea of what it’s really like down there. I can’t believe there’s stations that were built down there but never connected to the surface.

The foresight to build such a system so long ago is what makes a city like Paris great and why a city like Brisbane will never be on par.
