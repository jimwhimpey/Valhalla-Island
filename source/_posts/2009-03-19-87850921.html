Earth Hour exists so that people who do nothing can feel like they’re doing something. Drive home from work in your 4X4, turn on the air conditioning, watch programs on your massive TV, leave it on while you have a 30 minute shower, turn off your lights for an hour, feel good about the real difference you’re making to the environment.

An advertisement in the local paper read: “Join the Redland City Council as it turns off all non-essential lighting for Earth Hour”. How about turning off non-essential lighting all the time?
