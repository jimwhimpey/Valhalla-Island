iPhone 3G S Guided Tour Video

Samsung and other mobile phone manufacturers sell their phones using visual gimmicks, celebrities and treat actually using the phone as beside the point. It’s a testament to the iPhone that its marketing video doubles as an instructional video. The same goes for the huge majority of iPhone TV spots, showing how it works sells the device, imagine that!

Make a great product and the marketing takes care of itself.
