My new ride. A never ridden, 2006, Trek Madone sporting the Dura-Ace 7800 groupset. It’s the kind of bike pros are still riding today and it was just luck and good timing that I was able to get it at a price within my budget. It was clear after only riding it the 5km home from the bike store that it’s lightyears ahead of anything I’ve ridden before.

For now I’m using quite a high raised stem to slowly ease my spine back into cycling.
